THE COTTAGE

Our simple, but lovely cottage sleeps up to 4 people  (1 x double, 2 x single). We built the farm cottage ourselves using sandbags as an alternative to conventional building methods. The wooden furniture, doors, floors, ceilings and wood panelling were all custom made using alien trees growing on and nearby the farm.


The cottage consists of a bedroom and upstairs loft, bathroom (with shower), well equipped kitchen and living room with a large fireplace. You can also enjoy an evening braai on the outside patio — freshly baked bread and home-grown garden salad available on request.


The cottage (purposefully) has no electricity, but does have a gas water heater, stove and fridge as well as candles, for your convenience. We believe that in order to truly enjoy our natural surroundings, we need to temporarily escape at least some modern luxuries.


During the day, take a walk around the farm, through our small piece of mainly indigenous forest, go for a swim in our dam or just relax and wait for the sunset over the Tsitsikamma mountains.
